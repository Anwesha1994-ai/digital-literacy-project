# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for Students

## ✅ Do’s

- Use respectful and polite language while posting, commenting, or messaging others.  

- Share useful, informative, or positive content that reflects your interests or learning.  

- Keep your profile clean and professional, especially your bio and username.  

- Think carefully before posting anything, as it stays online for a long time.  

- Respect different opinions and be open to healthy discussions.  

- Use privacy settings wisely to control who can see your posts and personal information.  

- Give proper credit when sharing someone else’s work, ideas, or content.  


## ❌ Don’ts

- Don’t post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content that may hurt others.  

- Don’t spread fake news, rumours, or unverified information.  

- Don’t share sensitive personal details like phone number, address, or passwords.  

- Don’t engage in unnecessary online arguments or negative discussions.  

- Don’t upload or forward someone’s photos or videos without their permission.  

- Don’t waste too much time scrolling, as it can affect your studies and productivity.  

- Don’t react emotionally to every post; take time before responding.
